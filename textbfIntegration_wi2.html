Integration with Amazon CloudFront
AWS Elemental MediaStore seamlessly integrates with Amazon CloudFront, AWS’s content delivery network (CDN) service. This integration allows users to leverage CloudFront’s global edge locations for caching and delivering content, optimizing the distribution of media assets to end-users worldwide 11AWS Elemental.
